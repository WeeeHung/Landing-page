Nugget

Nugget is an effervescent, excitable and friendly boy who can sometimes get over excited. He enjoys his walks but would need to be guided on leash. Nugget is extremely perceptive and knows how to read your mood. He will only seek attention from you when he knows you are free.

History: Nugget was rescued as puppy.

Karen

Karen is a sweet and very affectionate girl who loves everyone and is good with other dogs. Call her and she will walk up to you with a smile plastered on her face. Give her a scratch on her head and you've got yourself a shadow who will follow you around.

History: Karen was born at Tuas South, one out of a litter of four.

Bruno

Bruno is a very handsome boy and looks like he could be a rottweiler cross. He possesses a happy and friendly nature and an extremely waggy tail! He is excellent indoors and is careful around furniture but he needs to know what is considered a toy and what is not!

History: Bruno was found abandoned in Punggol with heart worm and tick fever.

Dinky

Dinky is a happy-go-lucky dog who is eager to play with the humans he's familiar with. He knows his way into people's hearts, frequently asking for a good belly rub. He walks well on leash, but can be cheeky with inexperienced handlers.

History: Dinky was rescued from Tuas when he was a puppy.
